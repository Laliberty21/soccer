Real OOS (Out of Shape) is an over 30 coed soccer team that was created in the Spring of 2019. The team is comprised of 7 male players and 4 female players. In the inaugural season, Real OOS made it to the playoffs and went on to be the CHAMPIONS of the VSA Adult Soccer League located in Haymarket, Virginia.
