The facilities at James S. Long Regional Park include baseball and soccer fields, trails, basketball and tennis courts, a playground, picnic pavilions, t-ball fields, a mini-library, an equestrian ring and grass equestrian area. Public parking and restrooms are also available on-site. Additional information on James S. Long Regional Park can be found at Prince William County Parks
